Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

SurveyDXF is a field productivity tool for Android. The app is designed to collect only the information needed for licensing, support, and field workflows.

Information collected

SurveyDXF may collect or process:

  • Device ID used for trial and license activation.
  • Email address and customer name when provided during purchase or support.
  • License status, expiry date, payment reference, and last license check time.
  • Country code/name inferred from SIM, network, or locale for release planning.
  • DXF files, projects, stations, level-book entries, and captured points stored locally on the device.

How information is used

Information is used to provide trials, activate paid licenses, deter license abuse, support customers, and improve market availability decisions.

Local field data

Survey project data is stored locally on the device unless the user exports or shares it. SurveyDXF does not automatically upload DXF drawings or captured survey points for cloud processing.

Payment processing

Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy. Payment details are handled by Lemon Squeezy according to their policies. SurveyDXF stores only the payment metadata needed to activate and support the app license.

Data sharing

SurveyDXF does not sell personal data. Limited data may be processed through Supabase for licensing and Lemon Squeezy for payment.
